Julien KHAMPHOUSONE

Paris - Île-de-France, julien.khamphousone AT ensiie.fr

I am a contractual associate professor in combinatorial optimization at ENSIIE since the 1st of December 2025. I graduated from ENSIIE and MPRO in 2019. I hold a PhD in operations research from University Paris Dauphine and my supervisors were Pr. André Rossi, Dr. Sonia Toubaline and Dr. Fabian Castaño. I served one year as a post-doctoral researcher at Orange ending in 2024.
